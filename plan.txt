### **Project Title:** AetherIO: The Vision-Augmented Occupational Health Sentinel

**Tagline:** *"Don't just detect the smoke. Predict the fire."*

---

### **1. The Problem (The "Why")**

* **The Gap:** Traditional air safety systems (smoke detectors, air quality monitors) are **Reactive**. They only trigger an alarm *after* the air is already toxic. In high-stakes environments like chemistry labs, soldering stations, or 3D printing farms, this 30-second delay is dangerous.
* **The Pain Point:** Existing monitors are "blind." They see a spike in numbers but don't know *what* caused it (e.g., did someone spill a chemical or just open a marker?). This lack of context leads to false alarms and delayed ventilation.

### **2. The Solution (The "What")**

**AetherIO** is a **Multimodal Safety System** that combines **Computer Vision (Sight)** with **Environmental Sensing (Smell)** to create a predictive safety net.

* **Predictive Protection:** By using a camera to detect hazardous activities (like rapid movement, soldering motions, or chemical pouring), AetherIO activates ventilation systems *pre-emptively*, before fumes even reach the sensor.
* **Edge-First Architecture:** Powered by the **Raspberry Pi 5**, all AI processing happens locally on the device. This ensures millisecond-latency response times and keeps data secure (no cloud lag).

---

### **3. System Architecture (The "How")**

#### **A. Hardware Layer**

1. **The Brain (Raspberry Pi 5):** The central edge computing node. It runs the Web Server, the Computer Vision Engine, and the Logic Core.
2. **The Eye (USB Webcam):** Monitors the workspace for activity.
3. **The Link (Direct Ethernet):** A hardwired, air-gapped connection between the Sentinel (Pi) and the Command Center (Laptop), ensuring reliability even in zero-internet zones.
4. **The Nose (Simulated/ESP32):** A sensor module (simulated for demo stability) that tracks Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs).

#### **B. Software Layer**

1. **Backend:** **Python FastAPI**. Chosen for its speed and asynchronous capabilities, allowing it to handle Vision processing and WebSockets simultaneously.
2. **AI Engine:** **OpenCV**. Uses differential motion detection and contour analysis to identify "High Activity" zones in real-time.
3. **Frontend:** **HTML5/CSS3 "Digital Twin" Dashboard**. A high-contrast, "Dark Mode" interface that visualizes invisible threats (Air Quality) alongside visible ones (Camera Feed).
4. **Protocol:** **WebSockets**. Pushes data to the dashboard instantly (0 latency), rather than waiting for the page to reload.
